Built for the Gig

SetClick isn't just a metronome — it's your entire click track workflow. Songs, setlists, and a live mode designed for real performance.

🎵

Song Library

Store every song with its BPM, time signature, key, subdivision, click sound, and performance notes. Your whole repertoire in one place.

📋

Setlist Manager

Drag songs into setlists for each gig. Duplicate, reorder, and export — so your whole band stays on the same page.

🔴

Live Mode

Full-screen performance view with a beat ring, BPM display, song progress, and next-song preview. Built to glance at on a dark stage.

🗣️

Voice Count-In

Spoken beat numbers during count-in — "One, two, three, four" — so the whole band knows exactly when to come in.

🥁

4 Click Sounds

Classic tick, woodblock, digital beep, or hi-hat. Each one synthesized with realistic attack and decay — no samples.

📳

Haptic Feedback

Feel every beat through your phone. Heavy pulse on the downbeat, light tap on the rest. Perfect for in-ear monitoring setups.

🔇

Count-Off Only

Get the count-in, then silence. For bands that want the tempo set but prefer to play without a click running the whole song.

⏱️

Auto-Advance

Set song durations and the click auto-stops when time's up. See a live countdown so you always know where you are in the set.

🎛️

Lock Screen Controls

Play, pause, skip forward, skip back — all from the lock screen and Now Playing widget. No need to unlock mid-performance.

👆

Tap Tempo

Tap to detect BPM on the fly. Great for figuring out a song's tempo by feel — tap a few times and it locks in.

🔑

Song Keys

Tag every song with its key — major or minor. Visible in the song list, setlist detail, and live mode at a glance.

📤

Import & Export

Share setlists as JSON files. Export a backup of your entire library, or import one from a bandmate. Zero data lost between devices.

How It Works

1

Add your songs

Create each song with its BPM, time signature, key, click sound, and any performance notes. Tap tempo if you need to find the BPM by feel.

2

Build a setlist

Pick your songs, set the order. Each setlist is a self-contained gig plan — duplicate it for tomorrow's show with different songs.

3

Hit Live Mode

One tap and you're performing. The beat ring lights up, the count-in starts, haptics kick in. Skip forward and back between songs without leaving the screen.

4

Play the gig

Screen stays on, lock screen controls work, audio runs in the background. SetClick stays out of your way and keeps the band locked in.
