Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

SetClick is built with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

SetClick collects no data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No network requests are made by the app
  • No data is shared with third parties

Local Storage

All your songs, setlists, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the app's built-in export feature.

Export & Import

SetClick allows you to export and import setlists as JSON files. These files are created and managed entirely by you — the app does not upload them anywhere. Sharing is done through iOS's native share sheet, which you control.

Children's Privacy

SetClick does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
